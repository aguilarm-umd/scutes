About

Scutes is an open source email processing and personal identification information redacting web application for the preservation of White House Correspondents’ Association’s Pool Reports Collection.

What are Scutes?

Scutes are the keratinous plates on a turtle shell. They are similar to scales.

General reasons for choosing this name:

  • This name is in line with other software projects at UMD that use names related to turtles.
  • It is short and easy to say.
  • As far as we know, it is not used by another software developer or institution.
  • Although it has conceptual links to the project, it does not pigeon hole it to a limited set of features.

Some conceptual reasons for choosing this name:

  • Scutes are a protective layer; the app will act as protection for reporters by redacting personal information.
  • Scutes can help in the identification of turtles; the app will identify which email messages should be published.
